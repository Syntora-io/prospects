### Estimating Process (Primary Focus)
- Specialty contractor (acoustical ceilings + walls). Only prices their scope from full drawing sets.
- **Workflow:** Drawings received -> Plan Swift takeoff (click corners, measure distances) -> manual entry into Excel (pricing, markup, labor) -> PDF quote to customer
- **Time:** Average ~1 hour per project. Range: 20-30 min (small) to 8 hours (large jobs like universities)
- **Team:** 3 estimators
- **Core pain:** Flipping through pages to find only their scope. Missing items means standing behind a wrong quote.
- **AI goal:** Safety net that reviews drawings, spot-checks takeoffs, flags missed areas. "I found 16 areas similar to this -- you only found 14."
- Plan Swift used ~10-15 years, but not locked in.

### Other Departments (Secondary)
