The Situation

Edge Acoustics estimates every project by hand. An estimator opens the drawings in Plan Swift, clicks through every page to find acoustical scope, measures quantities, then manually types those numbers into Excel to build a quote. The process works, but it is slow, repetitive, and carries real financial risk when items are missed.

Your team tried to adopt AI internally and it didn't stick. That's not unusual. Most companies that skip structured discovery end up in the same place. The opportunity is real, but it needs to be scoped properly before anything gets built.

Before we build anything, we need to understand exactly how your estimators work, where the bottlenecks are, and which parts of the process AI can realistically improve. That is what this discovery phase delivers.

What Discovery Looks Like

Two focused calls with your head estimator to capture how the work actually gets done. One presentation call to walk you through everything we found and where AI and automation can make a measurable difference.

What We Investigate

  • Drawing review process -- how estimators identify acoustical scope across multi-discipline drawing sets
  • Plan Swift workflow -- takeoff process, measurement methods, output format, integration capabilities
  • Excel pricing workflow -- how quantities become quotes, markup calculations, labor pricing
  • Error patterns -- where and why items get missed, how that impacts the business
  • Drawing formats -- file types received, quality, consistency across customers and general contractors
  • Integration landscape -- Plan Swift, Google Workspace, QuickBooks, TSheets connections and data flows
