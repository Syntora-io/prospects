## Competitive Advantage

Most cleaning companies only cover standard home services directories. The gap is in Crunchbase, Wikidata, data aggregators, structured schema markup, and Trustpilot. Virtually no residential cleaning companies are listed on these. This creates an entity authority gap that competitors cannot easily replicate.

Gosia wants to be "first in the cleaning industry" with AI. She sits on the ARCSI board and offered to refer Parker to peers once results are proven. Getting directory saturation right early creates a moat that referral clients can replicate.
